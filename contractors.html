Frequently Asked Questions

What types of leads will I receive? +
All leads are for flat or low-slope roof replacement projects — commercial, industrial, and institutional buildings. Each lead includes the property address, roof size, material type, condition assessment, known issues, and the property owner's contact information with budget expectations.
How many contractors receive each lead? +
We limit lead distribution to a small number of contractors per project to give each one a meaningful chance of winning the work. Unlike large lead aggregators that sell to 10+ contractors, we focus on quality over quantity.
Is there a contract or commitment? +
No. Pay-per-lead has zero commitment — you only pay for leads you choose to unlock. Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled anytime. Annual subscriptions include a 30-day free trial so you can evaluate the quality of leads before committing.
What areas do you cover? +
We currently focus on major Canadian cities including Toronto, Vancouver, Calgary, Ottawa, Montreal, Edmonton, Winnipeg, Hamilton, Mississauga, and Brampton. We're expanding to additional cities — register now to be first in your area.
Do you take a percentage of my contracts? +
Absolutely not. You pay only for the lead (or subscribe for unlimited). Once you connect with the property owner, your quote and contract are between you and them. We never take a cut of your work.
